Duration Range

For the IDF and PDF curves, the duration range represents the time range along the X (horizontal) axis of the graph. It is important to select this option and the Intensity Units option correctly (if present) to avoid very odd output.

Options:

  • 5min-120min
  • 1hr-48hr
  • 1day-10day
