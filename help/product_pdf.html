Precipitation Frequency Duration Graphs

Precipitation Frequency Duration (also known as Precipitation Duration Frequency or PDF) curves show extreme rainfall amount over a range of durations (subhourly, hourly, or daily) for a constant recurrence interval.

This product shows preciptations amounts for base durations and recurrence intervals as symbols,and provides a simple graphical (strighht-line) interpolation for intermediate durations that are not specifically calculated in this project. Given the range of amounts and durations depicted by the graph logarithmic axes are used. A table under the graph shows the calculated and estimated values for each duration.
