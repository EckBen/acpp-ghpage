Submit

When you are ready to retrieve the requested data from the server, click the submit button. This will cause either a popup window, new tab, or browser download window to appear (depending on the product and delivery options).

The submit button dynamically changes colors to indicate if all required information has been input. When the submit button is red, you will not be able to submit the product for processing. When it turns green, all necessary information has been entered (or defaults will be used) and the user can retrieve their desired results.

Red Submit Button - Disabled

Green Submit Button - Good to go!
