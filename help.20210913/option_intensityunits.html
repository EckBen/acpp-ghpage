Intensity Units

For the IDF curves product, this option represents the units (inches per minute, inches per hour, or inches per day) of the precipitation intensity along the Y axis. It is important to sync this to (or at least select it strategically along with) the Duration Range option.

Options:

  • inches/minute
  • inches/hour
  • inches/day
