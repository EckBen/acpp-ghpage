Annual Maximum Series - by Station


In computing the extreme precipitation statistics in this project, we have utilized a Annual Maximum Series (AMS) to model the entire precipitation record. For each year in a station record, the highest precipitation amount is included in the AMS. This series is then fitted using the Gumbel distribution to determine the return period storms for each recurrence.

This product will retrieve the Annual Maximum Series for any station used in the final analysis. Please note, this product does not simulate or model point-specific data. Users are only able to select specific stations used in the analysis from the map interface. After submitting, a new window will display the Annual Maximum Series for both sub-hourly (5, 10, 15 and 30 minute) and hourly (1, 2, 6, 12, and 24 hour) events. The year in which each event occurred is also included.
