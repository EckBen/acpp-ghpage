Confidence Limits

This option will display the lower and upper confidence limit bounds on the product output. The confidence limits calculated here are 90% intervals, and the lower and upper bounds represent the 5th and 95th percentile respectively. These bounds were calculated via a Monte Carlo approach with 1,000 trials, then the intervals were gridded and smoothed across the entire region.
