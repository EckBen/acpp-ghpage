Partial Duration Series - by Point

In computing the extreme precipitation statistics in this project, we have utilized a Partial Duration Series (PDS) to model the entire precipitation record. For n complete years in a station record, the n highest values comprise the PDS. This series is then pushed into the Beta-P statistical distribution to determine the return period storms for each recurrence.

This product will retrieve the Partial Duration Series for any station used in the final analysis. Please note, this product does not simulate or model point-specific data. When a location is selected, the nearest station is selected and displayed.

For more control over which PDS to view, use the Partial Duration Series – by Station product.

Only selected durations are available, as some duration periods (such as the subhourly times – 5min, 10min, etc.) are ratio-driven calculations based on other duration return periods.
